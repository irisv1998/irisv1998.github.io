Guerrero Mexico

Enjoy!

Located in southwestern Mexico, Guerrero is known for its beautiful beaches, resort towns, and Pre-Columbian sites one of the top tourist destination states in Mexico.

Guerrero has a long recorded history, dating back to 1,000 BC and continuing through the Aztec Empire of the AD 1400s. Most of my family was born and raised in the state of Guerrero on my mothers side. I have visited many times and have always felt like home when I go. Guerrero has many beaches but my favorite is Zihuatanejo.

Short history of Guerrero and its surrounding cities. As well on Vicente Guerrero which the state is named after.

From the renowned beaches of Acapulco and Ixtapa to the silversmiths of Taxco, Guerrero is known as a mecca for ocean-loving tourists and sports fisherman. Vicente was a general in Mexico's war of independence, he helped write the nation's constitution, and he abolished slavery during his presidential administration. Vicente Guerrero of Mexico was of mixed African descent.
